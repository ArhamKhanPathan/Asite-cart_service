The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Snapdeal.As of March 2017, Flipkart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. In August 2018, American retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in Flipkart for US$16 billion, valuing Flipkart at around US$20 billion. Flipkart is valued at $37.6 billion as of 2022.
